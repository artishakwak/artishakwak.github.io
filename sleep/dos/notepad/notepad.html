take notes

ever feel like you're missing something or that if you fall asleep you
will forget to complete an important task? well, put your mind at
ease by simply jotting down notes on a piece of paper. write down
all your worries, ideas, exciting events of the day, and to do lists
then set them aside. not only will it serve as a reminder but it will
also purge your system of all your worries of the day.

benefits

  • relaxation
  • decreases stress
  • reassurance

verdict

  • keep a notepad on your nightstand
  • avoid taking notes right before bed
  • put aside 10-15mins of the day to
    write down notes
