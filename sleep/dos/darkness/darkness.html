darkness

darkness is the key to success...or at least when it comes to falling
asleep. darkness provides reprieve to tired eyes. Too much light,
right before bedtime may prevent you from getting a restful night
of sleep. In fact, one study recently found that exposure to unnatural
light cycles may have extreme consequences on our health, including
yet not limited to, increased risk of depression. Regulating exposure
to light is an effective way to maintain our normal internal processes.

benefits

  • better cycles of deep sleep
  • peaceful environment
  • less stimulation
  • horomones combating hunger
    are released

verdict

  • wear an eye mask to bed
  • turn off all the lights in the room
