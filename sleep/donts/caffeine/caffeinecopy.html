caffeine

caffeine has been considered the most popular drug in the world.
because caffeine is a stimulant, most people use it after waking up
in the morning or to remain alert during the day. although it is
important to note that caffeine cannot replace sleep, it can
temporarily make us feel more alert by blocking sleep inducing
chemicals in the brain and increasing adrenaline production.

prime suspects

  • coffee
  • tea
  • soda

criminal effects

  • headaches
  • stimulant
  • jitters

verdict

stop consuming caffeine a minimum
of 6hrs before bedtime.
