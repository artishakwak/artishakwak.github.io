night cravings

late night snacking is oh so tempting, but do you really want to wake
bae up with a cloud of onion breath?

prime suspects

  • self-denial of pleasurable food
  • nutritional imbalance
  • loneliness
  • grief
  • stress
  • temptation

criminal offenses

  • disruption of body clock
  • slower performance rate
  • type 2 diabetes
  • weight gain
  • binge eating

verdict

  • every once in a while treat yourself
    to healthy, delicious substitutes to
    everyday indulgences
  • drink plenty of water (think like a
    camel, be the camel)
  • eat breakfast (start off strong and
    you'll be less likely to cave)
  • distract yourself from eating
  • brush your teeth (we're all too lazy
    to do it again)
  • avoid snacking at least a minimum of
    3 hours before bedtime
  • do NOT eat after 8pm
